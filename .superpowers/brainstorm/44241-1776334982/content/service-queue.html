Queue ordering logic

AutoServices sorted by checked_in_at timestamp within each staff lane. Oldest waiting service rises to the top. No manual reordering needed — it's just chronological. Data already exists on the walkins endpoint.

What "auto-adjusts" means

LiveDashboard refreshes every 10s. When a staff member completes a service, their lane clears and the next queued slot promotes to the top. Staff who become FREE turn green. No separate backend logic needed.

Implementation note

EasyThis is a pure frontend transform of the existing /appointments/walkins/active response. Group walkins by assigned_staff.id, sort each group by checked_in_at. No new API endpoint required.
